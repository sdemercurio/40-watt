It Begins with trust...

"Nobody cares how much you know until they know how much you care."

– Theodore Roosevelt

I develop leaders through coaching, consulting, and training. At Stripe, I led manager enablement globally and also oversaw executive coaching programs for managers. Prior to that, I launched and led Own The Room’s San Francisco Bay Area operation, building a team that developed the communication skills of professionals in Silicon Valley's top companies.


My approach to leadership and team dynamics were shaped by years of coaching one of the most challenging audiences in the world: teenagers (in high school basketball). It starts with trust, and once that’s earned, we can go further than we ever imagined.
