Helping leaders find their authentic voice.

"Nobody cares how much you know until they know how much you care."

– Theodore Roosevelt

Resonance. It’s what I felt in my early 20’s when I first volunteered as a basketball coach at the local high school. I’d tapped into something deeply meaningful to me that gave me joy, fulfillment, and even fearlessness. It had little to do with the sports side of coaching--it was about forming a connection with players and helping them improve as people.


Nearly 20 years later, it’s that same resonance I’m chasing as I launch my own coaching and professional training business.


I am an executive coach, corporate trainer, and consultant based in San Francisco. I specialize in helping leaders find their authentic voices (and “leader” has nothing to do with title). I’ve coached and trained C-Suite executives as well as individuals of all experience levels at several global organizations since 2014. Prior to this I spent 13 years in client facing roles--both in corporations and non-profit. More recently, I led training and development for managers at a pre-IPO fintech company based in San Francisco.


Just like I learned coaching student athletes earlier in my career, it starts with trust. Once that’s earned we can go further than we ever imagined.
